About The Data

These maps were derived from public data provided by the Vermont Center for Geograhic Information. I used 0.7m resolution DEM data to generate hillshade, 0.5m resolution Tree Canopy data to color the landscape, the E-911 data for roads and trails.

All styling done in QGIS on an underpowered laptop.

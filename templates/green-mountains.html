Green Mountain Maps

Winter in High Definition

The Northern Green Mountains are home to some of the best tree skiing this side of Hokkaido. For the first time, they can be explored in high definition maps with elevation resolution 10 times better than any previously available and with tree-type classification accurate to a half-meter.

Click on any map to view it in full resolution. Happy hunting.

About The Data

These maps were derived from public data provided by the Vermont Center for Geograhic Information. I used 0.7m resolution DEM data to generate hillshade, 0.5m resolution Tree Canopy data to color the landscape, the E-911 data for roads and trails.

All styling done in QGIS on an underpowered laptop.

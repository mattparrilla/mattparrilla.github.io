About The Data

These maps were derived from public data provided by the Vermont Center for Geograhic Information.

I used 0.7-meter resolution DEM data to generate hillshade, 0.7-meter resolution DSM data to give the map some of the texture of the trees, and 0.5-meter resolution tree canopy classification to color the landscape for coniferous or deciduous. The state's E-911 data was used for roads and trails.

All styling done in QGIS on my underpowered laptop.
